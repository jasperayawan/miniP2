Why Buy Local?

FRESHNESS AND QUALITY: Local products are typically fresher and of higher quality as they don't need to travel long distances to reach the consumer.

SUPPORTING LOCAL ECONOMY: By buying from local farmers and producers, you are supporting the local economy and helping to create jobs in your community.

ENVIRONMENTAL BENEFITS: Locally grown products have a smaller carbon footprint as they require less transportation and it lessens packaging waste reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT: Shopping at a farm-to-table marketplace provides an opportunity to connect with your community and learn more about where your food comes from.
