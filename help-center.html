A farm-to-table marketplace is an online platform that connects farmers and producers with consumers directly. It enables consumers to purchase fresh, locally-grown or made products directly from the source.

Once you've created an account, you can browse products, add items to your cart, and place an order. The farmer or producer will receive the order and prepare your items for pickup or delivery. You will receive a notification once your order is ready.

All products listed on our website are sourced from local farmers and producers who adhere to high standards of quality and sustainability. We encourage transparency, so you can see information about the farmer or producer and their production methods on their product pages.

We accept major credit and debit cards for payment. Payment is processed at the time of checkout, and you will receive a confirmation email once the transaction is complete.

Modifications or cancellations may be possible depending on the status of the order. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible to inquire about any changes you need to make.

Pickup locations and delivery options will vary depending on the farmer or producer. Some may offer delivery directly to your home, while others may require pickup at a specific location. Delivery fees may apply.

If you have any issues with your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will work with you to resolve any issues and make sure you're satisfied with your purchase. Some farmers or producers may allow returns, while others may not due to the perishable nature of their products. Please see individual product pages for details.

We have a dedicate HELP CENTER who can address all your questions 24/7. Feel free to send an e-mail or call our accredited phone numbers.
